## Controls
Navigate with WASD. For sprinting, keep SHIFT pressed. To heal yourself press the H key. Switch between items by scrolling with your mouse. To attack, use your right mouse button, to interact, the left one. Press the right mouse button to close any menu and to pause the game, click ESC on your keyboard. To confirm selling or buying an item press ENTER. To switch between items while trading use W to go up and S to go down.
To open up the item menu, press B.
